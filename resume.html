Over the last 10 years I have worked toward becoming a well rounded marketer. I have worked professionally as an in-house photographer/artist, an in-house graphic designer, and (most recently) an email marketer working within Salesforce Marketing Cloud (SFMC).

In my career, I have been fortunate to work side by side with mentors who were passionate about passing their knowledge to their peers. I have worked my way from photographer to designer to overall marketer; gaining skills in print production, promotional item production, large scale signage, email design/development, campaign strategy/implementation, automated email processes, consumer segmentation via SQL, web/landing page development.

Some of my most recent career accomplishments are: Implementation of Abandoned Cart email series, Implementation of email signup via SMS program, creation of SFMC landing pages for social media giveaways, creation of branding/website/various printed materials for local political campaign.
